Discover More About TeleVue

Vision

We offer hassle-free transactions at your doorstep. Customer satisfaction is at the core of what we do, and we’re committed to making the process as smooth as possible. After receiving a quote, you can schedule a convenient pickup time and choose your preferred payment method.

Our professional team ensures a seamless experience, so you get maximum value and convenience when buying or selling with us.

Mission

To become India’s top choice for television recycling and resale, offering excellent value for used TVs while promoting sustainable practices by preventing electronics from ending up in landfills.

We’re passionate about providing great deals on quality used televisions.

Our goal is to serve our customers with integrity, value, and reliability.

Who We Are?

TeleVue is guided by a commitment to customer satisfaction, sustainable practices, innovative service, and long-term growth. We focus on delivering value to our customers rather than just competing with others in the industry. Founded over two decades ago, TeleVue began with a mission to revolutionize home entertainment. As pioneers in high-definition technology, we have been at the forefront of delivering cutting-edge television experiences. Our products are designed with both quality and environmental sustainability in mind, ensuring that our customers enjoy the best of both worlds. Over the years, TeleVue has grown into a trusted name, with a focus on providing reliable and innovative solutions for every home. Our dedication to quality and our customers continues to drive us forward.

How Televue Works?

Check Price

Choose the brand & provide your current television conditions in just a few steps and our Smart System will suggest the perfect and best price for your old and used television.

Schedule Pickup

Just let us know your convenient place (home or work) and the right time slot you want us to pick up your old television. Our executive will visit your place at the right time.

Get Paid

We pay you instantly when our executive picks up the television from your place. We promise an instant payment.

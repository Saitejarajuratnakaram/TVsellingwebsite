Return Policy

At TeleVue, we strive to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we are happy to assist you with returns in accordance with our Return Policy outlined below.

1. Return Eligibility

You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following criteria:

  • The item must be in its original, unused, and unopened condition.
  • The item must be returned in the original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
  • The item must not be damaged or altered in any way.
2. Non-Returnable Items

Some products are not eligible for return, including but not limited to:

  • Opened or used TVs and electronics, unless defective.
  • Custom or personalized items.
  • Gift cards and downloadable software products.
3. How to Return an Item

If you wish to return a product, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team at [Insert Contact Email] or [Insert Contact Phone Number] to initiate a return request.
  • Provide your order number, product details, and reason for the return.
  • Our team will send you a return authorization and return instructions.
  • Ship the item back to us using the return shipping label provided, along with a copy of the return authorization.
4. Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it meets the return criteria. If the return is approved, your refund will be processed to the original method of payment within 7-10 business days. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable, and you will be responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or an error occurred in the fulfillment of your order.

5. Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different model, color, or size, you will need to return the original item and place a new order for the desired product. Please follow the return and reordering steps outlined above.
